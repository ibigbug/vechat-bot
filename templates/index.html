How to Use

  1. Login with Google
  2. Create a Telegram bot using @BotFather on telegram
  3. Get the token and a name, fill in the form and click `submit`(Note: the name you submitted will be used in the synced chat)
  4. Click `toggle` to enable your Telegram bot
  5. Click `link to wechat` and login a wechat account which you want to sync
  6. Get back to this page and refresh after several seconds, you'll se you Telegram bot is linked to your wechat account
  7. Now, messages sent to your wechat will also be sent to your Telegram bot
  8. To reply a wechat message, use the Telegram `reply` feature
